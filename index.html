About Me


Hi there! My name is Ricson B. Escalicas. I'm a virtual assistant from Cebu, Philippines. I believe in a saying that "How you do anything is how you do everything." I always do my best in everything that I do. I work on improving myself along with my Core Values (My Character), Vision (The Man I Envsion To Be), and Mission (My Deemed Purpose In Life) collectively known as the Basis or just simply who I am as a person. With these clear goals in mind, I plan the things that I want to do and work on those plans every day!


Although the future in itself is unpredictable, it's becoming apparent through the consistent steps we take at the present moment, if God willing. I hope that one day, I'd be able to reach most if not all of my goals, so long as I act consistently and do the things I said to myself.
